Biography

Ramakrishnan T.B., widely known as Ramki, is one of Kerala's foremost financial market professionals — SEBI-registered Research Analyst (Reg. No. INH000010496) with a Post Graduate Program in Portfolio Management from NISM.

As MD of Sharewealth Securities Limited, he leads one of South India's premier brokerage groups from Thrissur — 220+ branches nationwide, NSE, BSE, MCX memberships and CDSL DP status.

Treasurer and Governing Council Member at Cochin Stock Exchange (1998–2000) and Kerala Regional Head of Sharekhan (2002–2006).
